The Current Trip Pool

Introduction

Now that you have the Cargo Simulator installed as explained in Setting up the Cargo Trust Simulator you will need to understand the trip pool, especially if you want to create new Scenarios

For the sake of simplicity and portability the different routes are hard coded into the application. It would be trivial to create a local reading of routes, but due to time concerns this feature is left in the future work.

Current Trips pool Contents

The current trip pool contains 8 different routes in the following order

  • 0 through 4 are route0
  • 5 through 8 are route1
  • 8 through 15 are route2
  • 15 through 17 are route3
  • 17 through 21 are route4
  • 21 through 121 are routes 0 through 7 in round robin fashion
